About Us

About Our Journey

Welcome to SampadaVR Specialty Eye Care, the premiere specialty center in the Malabar region and a beacon of hope for those suffering from Vitreoretinal Diseases. Located in the heart of Tirur, Mallapuram district, SampadaVR stands as a distinguished Tirur eye hospital, extending expert care to patients from Kozhikode, Wayanad, Palakkad, and Thrissur.

At SampadaVR, we blend the rich heritage of “Sampada” with cutting-edge “VR” (Vitreoretina and Virtual Reality) technology, reflecting our commitment to integrating traditional values with the latest in medical and technological advancements. Our center is not merely a clinic but a boutique consultation and surgery center, meticulously designed to provide the best patient experience, emphasizing comfort and well-being. Specializing in the unique needs of the most vulnerable, including elderly patients and premature babies, SampadaVR offers a serene and supportive environment, setting us apart from other Tirur eye hospitals.

Our pioneering spirit in embracing Virtual Reality and advanced technologies such as AI, machine learning, and deep learning in ophthalmology underscores our forward-looking approach. These innovations promise to revolutionize retinal care, offering new horizons in diagnosis, treatment, and patient experience. Our logo, representing the retina’s three zones in Retinopathy of Prematurity (ROP) screening, symbolizes our dedication to excellence and the integration of cutting-edge engineering advancements to enhance care quality.

SampadaVR Specialty Eye Care is more than a center; it’s a sanctuary where the latest developments in Medicine and Engineering converge to provide unmatched care. From Tirur to the broader Malabar region, we are committed to enlightening lives through compassionate, innovative care, ensuring every patient steps into a brighter, healthier future.
