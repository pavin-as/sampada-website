Your questions answered

Common questions

What are Vitreoretinal diseases?

Vitreoretinal diseases are conditions that affect the vitreous and retina of the eye, which can lead to vision impairment or loss. Common examples include diabetic retinopathy, retinal detachment, and macular degeneration.

How do I know if I need to see a Vitreoretinal specialist?

If you experience symptoms such as sudden vision loss, floaters, flashes of light, or a shadow appearing in your field of vision, it’s crucial to consult a vitreoretinal specialist immediately.

What technologies are used in diagnosing and treating retinal diseases at Sampada VR?

At Sampada VR, we are committed to utilizing the forefront of medical technology to diagnose and treat vitreoretinal diseases. , Apart from  surgical techniques for retinal repair, our arsenal of advanced equipment includes, but is not limited to:

  • Laser Treatment: We employ precision laser therapy for a variety of retinal conditions, offering minimally invasive solutions with optimal outcomes.
  • Humphrey Field Analyzer: A critical tool in our diagnostic suite, the Humphrey Field Analyzer allows us to assess the visual field, crucial for diagnosing and managing glaucoma alongside retinal diseases.
  • OCT: This state-of-the-art imaging system is integral to our practice, capable of performing a wide range of imaging required for comprehensive assessment of retinal diseases, glaucoma, and corneal conditions. Its multifunctional capabilities allow for detailed visualization and measurement, aiding in accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plans.

Can Diabetic eye diseases be treated?

Yes, with early detection and proper management, treatments such as laser surgery, vitrectomy, and injections can effectively control the progression of diabetic eye diseases and preserve vision.

What is tele-consultation and how does it work?

Tele-consultation allows patients to consult with our specialized national and international experts remotely, using digital communication tools. It’s especially beneficial for follow-up visits or for patients with mobility issues.

What makes Sampada VR different from other eye care centers?

Sampada VR is a boutique consultation and surgery center, focusing exclusively on vitreoretinal diseases. We offer a holistic approach to patient care, combining the latest technologies with a compassionate, patient-centric service model.

Do you offer services for children and elderly patients?

Yes, our center is designed to cater to patients of all ages, including the unique needs of elderly patients and premature babies, ensuring a comfortable and stress-free experience for our most vulnerable patients.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked directly through our website, by phone, or in person at our clinic. We also offer the convenience of booking tele-consultation appointments online.

What is the Retinopathy of Prematurity clinic at Sampada VR and how does it support affected infants?

The Retinopathy of Prematurity (ROP) clinic at Sampada VR Specialty Eye Care is dedicated to the early detection and treatment of ROP, a potentially blinding condition affecting premature infants. Our clinic is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools and treatment methods to ensure the best possible outcomes. Through comprehensive screenings, expert evaluations, and timely interventions, we strive to preserve and protect the vision of our youngest patients, offering hope and support to families navigating this challenging condition.

How does the Myopia Clinic at Sampada VR help manage and treat myopia in children and adults?

Our Myopia Clinic at Sampada VR Specialty Eye Care focuses on the specialized management and treatment of myopia (nearsightedness) in both children and adults. Utilizing advanced diagnostic tools, we assess the progression of myopia and implement personalized treatment strategies, including orthokeratology (ortho-k) lenses, multifocal contact lenses, and atropine eye drops. Our goal is to slow myopia progression, thereby reducing the risk of associated complications later in life. Our dedicated team provides education, support, and innovative solutions to help our patients achieve optimal visual health.

How can I prepare for my first visit?

Please bring any previous medical records related to your eye health, a list of current medications. .Arriving 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork is also recommended.
